Welcome to the Mobile Pitch Deck Converter

About our project

The Mobile Pitch Deck Converter is a revolutionary tool designed to assist startups in creating accessible, mobile-friendly versions of their pitch decks. By simply uploading your current deck, the converter extracts the key elements, analyzes the content using NLP techniques, and generates a mobile-optimized version. This ensures your compelling pitch can be accessed and fully appreciated anytime, anywhere, on any device.

How it works

  1. Click on 'Choose a file' button and select your pitch deck file.
  2. Once a file is selected, the 'Upload' button will be enabled.
  3. Click on the 'Upload' button to start the conversion process.
  4. Wait for the conversion to complete. Do not refresh or close the page during this time.
  5. Once the conversion is complete, you will be redirected to a page to view and download your mobile-optimized pitch deck.
